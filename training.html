Our Internship & Training Programs

Organizational / Applied Psychology Internships

Industrial & Organizational Psychology Internship

Focus on workplace assessments, leadership, motivation, stress management, team dynamics.

HR Psychology Internship

Psychometric testing, behavioral interview training, employee engagement, and performance evaluation techniques.

Life Skills & Soft Skills Training Internship

Public speaking, emotional intelligence, time management, stress reduction workshops.

Clinical & Counseling Psychology Internships

Basic Counselling Skills Internship

Training interns in active listening, empathy building, rapport formation, questioning, and ethical practices.

Applied Counselling Internship

Case discussions, role plays, mock sessions under supervision, exposure to real client observation (if permissible).

Therapy Approaches Internship

Introducing CBT, REBT, Mindfulness, Positive Psychology, Art Therapy, etc. through demonstrations and practice exercises.

Mental Health Awareness Internship

Training interns to conduct psychoeducation sessions, awareness workshops, and mental health campaigns.

Specialized Internships (Niche & Value-Adding)

Grief & Trauma Counselling Internship

Skills in handling trauma cases, grief models, resilience-building strategies.

Child & Adolescent Psychology Internship

Child behavior observation, play therapy, parenting workshops.

Clinical Case Study Internship

Exposure to real/curated cases, building diagnostic skills, DSM/ICD awareness.

Community Psychology Internship

Working on mental health in underprivileged communities, awareness campaigns, workshops.

Research & Academic Internships

Psychology Research Internship

Training interns in research methods, data collection, analysis, report writing, and APA style referencing.

Testing & Assessment Internship

Hands-on training with psychological tools (intelligence, personality, aptitude, career interest).

Educational Psychology Internship

Classroom observation, learning difficulties, counseling in academic settings.
